Discover Chhattisgarh

Chhattisgarh sits in the center of India and is rich in dense forests, cascading waterfalls, ancient temples, and a vibrant tribal culture. Created in 2000, the state quickly became known for its breathtaking natural beauty — from the wide Chitrakote Falls to the deep caves of Kanger Valley. A paradise for eco-tourists and cultural travellers alike.

The region preserves several indigenous traditions, folk arts, and festivals. Iconic destinations include Jagdalpur, Bastar, Raipur, Bilaspur, and Dantewada. The state is rich in biodiversity, with national parks and sanctuaries that protect tigers, leopards, deer, langurs and many bird species.

Spiritual seekers will find ancient shrines like the Danteshwari and Bhoramdeo temples, while adventure lovers can explore caves, rock formations, and tribal markets. For food lovers, local cuisine includes steamed rice delicacies, sweets, and unique tribal flavours that can’t be found elsewhere.

Whether you seek wild nature, colourful tribal culture, historic temples or peaceful retreats, Chhattisgarh promises an authentic Indian experience away from the usual tourist crowds.
