Delhi

India’s Capital — History, Monuments & Urban Culture

Discover Delhi

Delhi, the national capital territory, is a blend of ancient monuments, colonial-era architecture, bustling bazaars, and modern urban life. From the Mughal grandeur of the Red Fort and Humayun’s Tomb to the colonial Rajpath and India Gate, Delhi tells India’s history in stone and streets.

The city also offers world-class museums, thriving arts and food scenes, and quiet heritage pockets like Hauz Khas and Mehrauli. Delhi is a gateway to India’s past and present — perfect for culture seekers, history buffs and food lovers.

Best time to visit: October–March. Expect busy tourist circuits around New Delhi and Old Delhi, and lesser-known heritage walks in Mehrauli and Shahdara.
